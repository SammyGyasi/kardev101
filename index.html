Highlights

Diverse Participation

This event will convene around 500 attendees, including heads of state, ministers, governors, high-ranking representatives from regional and multilateral economic development organizations, private sector corporations, and other development partners.

Exploring Business Opportunities

Central to the forum is the coming together of key players from business circles, governments, investors, entrepreneurs, and more. Discussions will encompass pivotal sectors such as agriculture, agro-industry, tourism, renewable energies, and the digital economy.

Promoting Sustainable Development

The forum ardently advocates for public-private partnerships, crucial for sustainable economic progress in Africa and the Mediterranean. Emphasis is placed on the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), focusing on responsible entrepreneurship and corporate social responsibility.

Innovation First

The forum also identiﬁes itself as a platform for young entrepreneurs, proactively encouraging their participation in innovative domains.

Networking and Partnerships

This event isn't just a high-level networking platform but also an opportunity to forge robust cooperation agreements among African nations.

Investment and Exchange Nexus

Strategically located in Tangier, where Africa meets the Mediterranean, the AfricaMed Business Forum stands as a central point for investments and trade. The overarching goal is to position Morocco as a pivotal player in Africa's economic growth.

The World Is Yours

AfricaMed Business Forum is an international event that will be held in Tangier on December 18-19, 2023. Its primary goal is to promote investment and economic development between Africa and the countries of the Mediterranean region. The forum will bring together approximately 500 high-profile public and private figures, including heads of state, ministers, political officials, representatives of international organizations responsible for economic development, private sector companies, and many regional actors. AfricaMed Business Forum will be a crucial gathering for entrepreneurs and investors from Africa, the Mediterranean, and the rest of the world.
